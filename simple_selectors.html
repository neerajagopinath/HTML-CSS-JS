Subheading 1 (h2)

Generation Z, or Gen Z, is the generation of people born between the late 1990s and early 2000s. They are the first generation to grow up with the internet and smartphones as a part of their daily lives.

Gen Z is the most diverse generation in American history. They value diversity and finding their own unique identities.

Subheading 2 (h2)

Gen Zers are pragmatic about how to address issues like climate change. They are also more willing to accommodate the failings of companies.
